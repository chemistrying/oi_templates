\subsection{Linked List}
\subsubsection{Singly Linked List}
To initiate a linked list:
\begin{lstlisting}
list<int> lst(5, 0)
\end{lstlisting}
To insert element \texttt{x} to index \texttt{idx}:
\begin{lstlisting}
auto it = lst.begin();
advance(it, idx);
lst.insert(it, x);
\end{lstlisting}
To erase element of index \texttt{idx};
\begin{lstlisting}
auto it = lst.begin();
advance(it, idx);
lst.erase(it);
\end{lstlisting}
